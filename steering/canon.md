# ICA canon — single source of truth

This site is an in-universe artifact of an existing running joke among friends. The source
material is in `evidence/` (gitignored — emails and a WhatsApp chat between Alicia Lake,
Tony Cullen, Douglas Ronne, and others, with Patrick "Pat" Thornenberg as the NCCWL's
fictional Chairman). All site copy must be consistent with that material. When in doubt,
consult `evidence/` before inventing new lore.

## Organizational identity

The user picked the domain `internationalcribbageassociation.org`, so the public-facing name
is the **International Cribbage Association (ICA)**. The evidence files use earlier and
parallel names:

- **NCCWL** — National Crib Champ World League (with variants "National Cribby Campy World
  League Association", "National Cribbage World League"). Pat Thornenberg signs as
  "NCCWL Chairmanship."
- **WICC** — invoked once as the body to bill postage to ("charge postage to the WICC")
- **IWCCA** — invoked once regarding a commissioned portrait for the "hall of Champion"

In-universe reconciliation: the **ICA is the umbrella international body**. NCCWL is its
**North American confederation**, of which Pat Thornenberg is the long-time Chairman.
WICC and IWCCA are subordinate committees (World International Cribbage Championship Council;
International World Cribbage Champions Association — Hall of Champions trustees). The acronym
proliferation is presented as the natural sediment of a 130-year-old institution.

## Founding & headquarters

- **Founded:** 1893, London, as the *Worshipful Society of Cribbage Players*
- **Renamed/reorg:** 1923 as the International Cribbage Association
- **Relocated:** 1946 to The Hague, Netherlands
- **HQ:** "Suckling House," Lange Voorhout, The Hague
- **Motto:** *Quindecim duo* — "fifteen, two"
- **Member federations:** 87 national associations across 6 confederations (European,
  Pan-American, African, Asian, Oceanic, and the Free Federation of Polar Cribbage Bodies)
