From the Executive Director

On behalf of the Secretariat and the eighty-seven member federations of the International Cribbage Association, I extend a sober welcome to the Association's official register of record.

The Association has, since its founding in the autumn of 1893, regarded the proper conduct of the game as a matter of the deepest institutional seriousness. It continues to do so today, in The Hague as in London before it, and in the eighty-seven national chanceries that report through this secretariat.

We commend to the visitor's attention our codified rules, our calendar of sanctioned events, and our official scoring engine — the instrument used by Tribunal adjudicators in all sanctioned competitions.

Yours in cribbage,
P. Thornenberg
Executive Director
